RAZZAQ SHIKALGAR

Email: president@apsit.in

Welcome to our college! I am delighted to share my thoughts with you. As we embark on this journey of learning and growth together, I encourage you to embrace every opportunity that comes your way. Our college is a vibrant community dedicated to fostering knowledge, creativity, and collaboration.

In these transformative years, you will encounter various challenges and triumphs. Remember that every challenge is an opportunity for growth. Lean on your peers, faculty, and the resources available to you. Together, we can create an environment that nurtures excellence and innovation.

Education is not just about acquiring knowledge; it's about building character, forging lifelong friendships, and discovering your passion. Take the time to explore various activities, join clubs, and engage in community service. These experiences will shape you into a well-rounded individual and prepare you for a successful future.

As you navigate your academic journey, I urge you to maintain a balance between your studies and extracurricular activities. Your mental and physical well-being is crucial, so don't hesitate to seek help when needed. Our college offers various support services to assist you in achieving your personal and academic goals.

I also want to highlight the importance of diversity and inclusion in our college community. Embrace the different perspectives and backgrounds of your fellow students. Together, we can create an enriching environment that fosters understanding and respect for all individuals.

I am here to support you and ensure that your college experience is enriching and fulfilling. Let's work together to make our college a better place for everyone, where ideas flourish and dreams become reality. I look forward to seeing all that you will achieve during your time here.
